## Deck Objective

Give judges or collaborators a complete, skimmable version of the pitch without replacing the 3-minute video. The deck should prove four things quickly:

1. The problem is real: the 2026 hybrid reset makes every lap an energy decision.
2. OVERRIDE works in practice: live cockpit, completed debrief, what-if, Fan Mode.
3. The AI is core and explainable: Granite reasoning, Docling grounding, Guardian scoring, TTM-R2 graceful forecast path.
4. The project is feasible: open-source, replay-first, TORCS/FastF1 data paths, 439 tests, containerized services.
