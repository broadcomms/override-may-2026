## May Innovation Challenge

Car racing is one of the most data-intensive, fast-paced environment in the world, where teams must process massive amounts of information and make critical decisions in real time. Inspired by the excitement of the F1 Grand Prix, this challenge invites you to apply AI improve how teams, drivers, or fans experience racing before, during, or after the race.

Your solution can focus on areas such as decision support, analytics, copilots, perception, or fan engagement, but it must use AI as core component along with at least one of the required technologies. Whether you are heling teams make better race-day decisions, turning complex telemetry into clear insights, or creating more engaging and understanding, and build trust through explainability.

### Building Your Solution and Examples
To build your solution, you must use at least one of the following technologies:
-	IBM Granite
-	Docling
-	Langflow
-	Context Forge

You can approach this challenge from multiple angles across the racing ecosystem. Consider building a solution in one of the following areas, or explore your own idea that aligns with the challenge:
-	AI Strategy & Decision Support
    o	Race strategy
    o	Pit stop optimization
    o	Performance recommendation
-	AI Analytics
    o	Telemetry analysis
    o	Lap performance insights
    o	Driver comparison tools
-	AI Copilot / Assistant
    o	Real-time feedback systems
    o	Race engineer assistants
-	AI Perception & Vision
    o	Track analysis
    o	Hazard detection
    o	Competitor modeling
-	Fan Experience
    o	AI Commentary
    o	Race explainer
    o	Interactive tools
